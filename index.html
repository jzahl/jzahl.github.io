I am an associate professor in the mathematics department at The University of British Columbia. I have a broad interest in classical harmonic analysis and combinatorics. I'm especially interested in incidence geometry, the restriction and Kakeya problems, and sum-product phenomena.

I am a member of the discrete mathematics and harmonic analysis groups at UBC, and I am involved with the discrete math seminar and the harmonic analysis seminar. If you would like to speak at either of these seminars, please contact me.

I am a co-organizer for the programme Frontiers in harmonic analysis, elliptic and parabolic PDEs, and GMT, which will take place at the Newton Institute in Cambridge UK from January - April 2027.
